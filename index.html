Privacy Policy for Orbit

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Controller and contact

Orbit is provided by Tim Lindenberger. For privacy questions, contact: orbit.offline.organizer@gmail.com.

Overview

Orbit is an offline organizer app. Your organizer content, including tasks, notes, lists, collections, attachments, reminders, and settings, is stored on your device. Orbit does not run a developer-operated server for your organizer data, does not create user accounts, does not include advertising or analytics SDKs, and does not sell personal data.

Data processed by Orbit

  • Content you create or import in the app.
  • Files, images, videos, and audio recordings you choose to add.
  • Reminder and notification settings.
  • In-app purchase status for voluntary support purchases, as provided by Google Play.

Permissions

Orbit requests permissions only for features you use. Camera and media access are used to add images or videos. Microphone access is used for audio recordings and speech input. Notification and alarm permissions are used for reminders. File and sharing features are used for import, export, and Orbitshare. Internet access may be used for Google Play purchases and for links that you choose to open; Orbit does not sync organizer data to developer-operated servers.

Sharing and third parties

Orbit does not send your organizer data to the developer. If you use import, export, sharing, Orbitshare, email, website links, or external files, data is transferred only to destinations you choose or to services handled by your device or the selected app.

In-app purchases

Voluntary support purchases are processed by Google Play. Payment information is handled by Google. Orbit does not receive complete payment details.

Storage and deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it in Orbit, clear the app data, or uninstall Orbit. Exported files and shared content are outside Orbit's control.

Security

Orbit stores data locally in the operating system's app sandbox. For protected content, device security features such as biometrics may be used if you enable them.
